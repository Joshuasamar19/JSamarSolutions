UKCC Center Project

Multidisciplinary clash detection for the UKCC Center, coordinating electrical, mechanical, and structural models to catch conflicts before they reached the field. Resolutions were reviewed directly with the design team through Autodesk Construction Cloud.

Challenge

A multidisciplinary build required catching conflicts between electrical, mechanical, and structural systems before they reached the field.

Approach

Ran systematic clash-detection reviews in Navisworks across all three disciplines, coordinating resolutions with the design team through Autodesk Construction Cloud.

Result

Kept the project ahead of schedule by resolving conflicts early, avoiding costly rework once construction began.

🔧 Behind the process

Merging electrical, mechanical, and structural models.

Clash Detection

Running clash-detection checks in Navisworks.

Team Review

Reviewing flagged clashes with the design team.

Resolution

Updating the model and confirming clashes are resolved.
